Academic Profile

Multimodal Generative Uncertainty

I am a master's student at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. My research focuses on uncertainty quantification for cross-modal generation in multimodal large models, especially uncertainty evaluation for text-to-image foundation models across diverse scenarios.

Primary Area Multimodal AI
Core Problem Generative Uncertainty
Application Focus Text-to-Image Models
Degree Timeline 2025-2028

Research

Research Focus

Cross-Modal Generative Uncertainty

Quantifying uncertainty when multimodal models translate textual conditions into visual outputs.

Text-to-Image Evaluation

Evaluating uncertainty, semantic alignment, and scenario-level robustness in text-to-image models.

Multimodal Model Reliability

Studying model behavior beyond surface-level visual quality when outputs are plausible but weakly grounded.
