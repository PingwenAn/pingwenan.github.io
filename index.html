About Me

I received my bachelor's degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2025. I am currently pursuing a master's degree at the School of Intelligent Engineering and Automation, BUPT, and expect to graduate in the summer of 2028.

My research interests include uncertainty evaluation for multimodal large models, with a particular focus on uncertainty in text-to-image generation models.
